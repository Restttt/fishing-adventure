Fishing Adventure

Pair our quick & easy 2-night cruise to Grand Bahama Island with an exciting fishing expedition on the glittering waters just off the coast. After indulging in all the activities our ship has to offer during your first and night on board, rise early on day 2 to take advantage of included transportation to the marina where your fishing boat awaits.

Meet your friendly captain and then set for the waters offshore, which are rich in a variety of game species such as sailfish, swordfish, marlin, mahi mahi, wahoo, and tuna, to name a few. You'll spend approximately 3.5 hours at sea angling for an amazing catch or simply relaxing on deck and absorbing the magical atmosphere of the sea. If you're not quite ready to go back to the ship after your fishing journy, enjoy extra time to shop, relax on the beach or take part in any of Grand Bahama Island's countless activities. Just check the Shore Excursions Desk aboard the ship for a variety of suggestions that will perfectly enhance your day in paradise.
